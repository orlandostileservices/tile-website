About Us

We are a general contractor specializing in tile installation and renovation throughout Auburn, Washington and the surrounding areas. Our team of skilled craftsmen uses modern techniques and high‑quality materials to deliver beautiful, long‑lasting results. From simple floor tile to custom showers, backsplashes and decorative patterns, we take pride in bringing your vision to life.

Quality workmanship, prompt service and client satisfaction are at the core of everything we do. Whether you’re updating a single room or completing a full remodel, we treat every project with the same attention to detail and professionalism.
