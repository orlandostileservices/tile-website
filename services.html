Our Services

Complete tile solutions for your home and business

What We Do

From concept to completion, we provide a full range of tile services tailored to your specific needs. Whether you're planning a luxury bathroom remodel, want to upgrade your kitchen backsplash, or need to repair damaged tile, our experienced team is ready to help. We work with ceramic, porcelain, natural stone and mosaic materials to create beautiful surfaces that stand the test of time.

Floor Tile Installation

We install tile floors that are both beautiful and resilient. From kitchens and bathrooms to entryways and living areas, we ensure level substrates, precise cuts and grout lines, and a flawless finish.

Backsplash & Bathroom Tile

Transform bathrooms and kitchens with custom tile designs. We specialize in shower surrounds, tub enclosures and backsplashes that add personality and protect your walls from moisture.

Tile Repair & Restoration

Cracked or loose tile? We repair damaged installations, replace tiles and restore grout to bring your surfaces back to life. We also offer deep cleaning and resealing to extend the life of your investment.

Grout Cleaning & Sealing

Over time grout can become stained and discoloured. Our cleaning and sealing process removes dirt and mildew, then applies a protective sealer to keep your tile looking its best.
