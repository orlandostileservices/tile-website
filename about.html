About Us

Craftsmanship, Experience & Integrity

Our Story

Tile Company began as a small family‑run business dedicated to providing exceptional tile work for homeowners in Auburn. Over the years we have grown into a trusted general contractor with a reputation for quality, creativity and professionalism. Our projects range from residential remodels to commercial installations, but our commitment to craftsmanship remains unchanged.

We believe every surface tells a story, and the materials you choose are just as important as the hands that install them. That’s why we work with some of Washington’s most talented designers and architects to deliver custom tile solutions that reflect your style and stand the test of time.

Our Values

  • Quality First: We never cut corners. Each project is installed to the highest standards, using proven techniques and premium materials.
  • Clear Communication: From the initial consultation to project completion, we keep you informed and involved every step of the way.
  • Respect & Reliability: We treat your home with the same care we would our own, arriving on time and leaving your space clean and safe.
  • Continuous Improvement: Our team stays up‑to‑date with the latest industry innovations so that we can offer you the best products and practices.
